PHO!!!!!

This is by far my most favorite meal of all time. It is a Vietnamese style noodle soup that consists of noodles, beef cuts, pork meat balls, beanprouts, various herbs and spices complemented with hoison sauce and sriracha!

The history of this palatable dish goes way back to the 1880s, during the unification of Vietnam under French rule. It is one of the most iconic dishes and has a deep heritage for Vietnamese folks. The word "pho" itself is an adaptation of the French word, "feu" meaning fire.

Today there are many restaurants that serve this dish, as well as variations which include chicken, pork, vegetarian, and a "pho dac biet", which is a combination of different meats and the best kind!
